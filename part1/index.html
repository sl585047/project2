Philadelphia Flyers History

How many Stanley Cups have the Flyers won and when?

The Flyers have only won 2 Stanley Cups, they were won in 1974 and 1975 (clearly it's time for a win)

How many players are in the Flyers Hall of Fame?

There are 18 players in the Flyers Hall of Fame. Total number of inductees is 24. The other inductees include coaches, owners, chairmen, and one broadcaster.

Who was inducted into the Hall of Fame this past season?

John Leclair (1994-2004), Eric Lindros (1992-2000), and Eric Desjardins (1994-2006).

What is the Legion of Doom and who played on it?

The Legion of Doom was a forward line for the Philadelphia Flyers who played together between 1995 and 1997 comprising center Eric Lindros, left winger John LeClair and right winger Mikael Renberg.

Who is the current general manager and did he ever play for the Flyers?

The current GM is Ron Hextall and yes he did play for the Flyers (1986-1992, 1994-1999) and is known as one of their best goaltenders also as one of the most aggressive. He holds the record for the most penalty minutes in a single season for a goaltender.
